OUR STORY

Why Our Diamonds Shine Brighter for Over 45 Years

At Pramukh Hari Diamonds, we believe in crafting diamonds that reflect timeless elegance, unmatched brilliance, and a legacy of trust for over 45 years.

  • More than 30 years experience in freshwater pearl cultivation and production. More than 25 years experience in foreign trade. Every year, we get the Sedex certificate.
  • We have our own design team, we make more than 20 new styles every week.We can make sample according to customer’s requirement, and can OEM.
  • We do business with customers all around the world. Some of our big customers are Forever21, hobby lobby, Barcs,Nordik Light,Blue Illusion.

Heritage Meets Innovation, Crafted by Visionaries From a foundation steeped in decades of diamond expertise, our journey began five years ago, led by a founder who saw beyond the ordinary. With 45 years of combined experience, our team has reimagined what it means to create diamonds, infusing centuries-old traditions with bold new ideas. Guided by the philosophy of "Heritage Meets Innovation," we believe that true luxury comes from combining history's finest techniques with tomorrow’s possibilities. Our diamonds reflect the spirit of visionaries who understand that the future is shaped by the passion of today’s artisans.

Why Choose Our Blinds

01

Customizable Designs

Our blinds come in a variety of styles, colors, and materials to match any interior decor. Whether you prefer sleek, modern designs or classic, timeless looks, we offer solutions that will complement your space beautifully.

02

Energy Efficiency

Our blinds help regulate indoor temperatures by providing insulation against the heat in summer and keeping warmth inside during winter. This can lead to energy savings and a more comfortable living environment.

03

Easy Maintenance

We understand that convenience matters. Our blinds are designed to be easy to clean and maintain, keeping your space looking fresh with minimal effort.

04

Privacy & Light Control

With our wide range of blinds, you can control the amount of light entering your space while maintaining your privacy. Whether you need total darkness for a good night's sleep or just a soft, natural light ambiance, our blinds offer the perfect solution.

05

Durability & Quality

Our blinds are crafted from high-quality materials that are built to last. Resistant to wear and tear, they offer long-term value and performance, making them a smart investment for any home or office.

06

Professional Installation

We provide expert installation services, ensuring that your blinds fit perfectly and function smoothly. Our team takes the time to get it right, leaving you with flawless results.
